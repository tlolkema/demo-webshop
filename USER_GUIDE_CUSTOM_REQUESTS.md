# Custom Product Request Feature - User Guide

## How to Use

### 1. Request a Product

**Step 1:** Click the red "📝 Request Product" button at the top of the product list.

**Step 2:** A modal dialog will appear with the title "Request a Product".

**Step 3:** Fill in the form:
- **Product Name** (required): Enter the name of the product you want to request
  - Example: "Siamese Cat", "Ragdoll", "Sphynx"
- **Description** (optional): Provide additional details about what you're looking for
  - Example: "Looking for a loyal and affectionate breed"
- **Reference Link** (optional): Share a link if you have a reference
  - Example: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siamese_cat"

**Step 4:** Click the "Add to Basket" button.

✅ **Result:** Your requested product appears in the shopping basket with a 📋 icon and "Customer Requested" badge.

---

### 2. View Requested Products in Basket

Navigate to [basket.html](basket.html) to view your shopping cart.

**Requested items display as:**
```
📋 Siamese Cat [Customer Requested] [Edit] [Remove]
```

**Styling:**
- Yellow/cream background (#fff8e1)
- Orange left border (4px)
- Orange "Customer Requested" badge
- Blue "Edit" and "Remove" buttons

**Regular items display as:**
```
🐈‍⬛ Maine Coon
```

---

### 3. Edit a Requested Product

**Step 1:** Click the "Edit" button next to the requested product in your basket.

**Step 2:** The request modal will reopen with your current data pre-filled.

**Step 3:** Modify any of the fields (name, description, or link).

**Step 4:** Click "Add to Basket" to save your changes.

✅ **Result:** The requested product in your basket is updated with the new information.

---

### 4. Remove a Requested Product

**Step 1:** Click the "Remove" button next to the requested product in your basket.

**Step 2:** A confirmation dialog will ask: "Remove this requested product?"

**Step 3:** Click "OK" to confirm deletion (or "Cancel" to keep it).

✅ **Result:** The requested product is removed from your basket immediately.

---

### 5. Close the Modal

You have three ways to close the request modal:

1. **Click the × button** in the top-right corner of the modal
2. **Click outside the modal** (on the dark background)
3. **Press the Escape key** on your keyboard

---

## Data Persistence

All your requested products are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. This means:
- ✅ Your requests persist if you refresh the page
- ✅ Your requests remain in the basket even after closing the browser
- ✅ Clearing your browser's cache will remove your requests

## Tips & Tricks

💡 **Tip 1:** Be as descriptive as possible in the description field. This helps the shop owner understand exactly what you're looking for.

💡 **Tip 2:** Use the reference link to share images, articles, or product pages that match what you want.

💡 **Tip 3:** You can request multiple products - just click "Request Product" again and add a new request.

💡 **Tip 4:** Edit your request if the shop owner asks for more details or clarification.

💡 **Tip 5:** Remove old requests once they've been fulfilled or if you change your mind.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I request multiple products?**
A: Yes! Click "Request Product" multiple times and add as many requests as you like.

**Q: What happens to my requested products?**
A: The shop owner can review them in the system and may contact you to discuss availability or alternatives.

**Q: Can I edit a request after adding it?**
A: Yes! Click the "Edit" button next to any requested product to modify it.

**Q: Will my requests be saved if I clear my browser?**
A: No. Clearing your browser's cache/storage will delete your requests. You may want to take a screenshot first.

**Q: Can I add regular products and requested products together?**
A: Yes! Both will appear in your basket side-by-side. Regular products show with their emoji, requested products show with a 📋 icon.
